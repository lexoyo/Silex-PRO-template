About Silex

Silex is an open source hackable website builder anyone can use to design beautiful responsive websites.

Professional designers can produce all types of websites quickly and use advanced features and integrations.

Agencies customize Silex to make it the most efficient tool for their domain and with their workflow.

Software editors add drag and drop capabilities to their platforms and provide their users a great UX to design content.
